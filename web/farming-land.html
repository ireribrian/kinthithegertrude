Our farm

Welcome to our school's farm, a dedicated space for hands-on agricultural projects and learning experiences. Spanning 3 acres, this farm is an integral part of our 6-acre school campus.

About Our Farm

Our farm provides students with the opportunity to engage in practical agricultural activities, learning about sustainable farming practices, crop cultivation, and livestock management. The farm is equipped with modern tools and facilities to support a variety of agricultural projects.

Agricultural Projects

On our 3-acre farm, students participate in projects such as:

  • Crop cultivation and management
  • Organic farming techniques
  • Soil improvement
  • Water conservation practices

Learning Outcomes

Through hands-on experience, students gain valuable knowledge and skills in agriculture, enhancing their understanding of food production and sustainability. These activities not only complement their academic studies but also foster a sense of responsibility and environmental stewardship.

Community Involvement

Our farm serves as a hub for community involvement, where local farmers, experts, and volunteers contribute to educational programs . This collaboration enriches the learning experience and strengthens the bond between our school and the community.
