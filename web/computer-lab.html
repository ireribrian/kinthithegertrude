Computer Laboratory Bio

Welcome to our state-of-the-art Computer Laboratory. Equipped with the latest technology and software, our computer lab provides students with a dynamic environment for learning and exploration in the world of computing.

Facilities and Resources

Our computer lab features:

  • Modern desktop computers
  • Printers and scanners for document processing
  • Interactive teachers for collaborative learning
  • Specialized software for various packages

Curriculum Integration

The computer lab is an integral part of our curriculum, supporting subjects such as:

  • microsoft office packages
  • Inroduction to computer languages
  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD)

Hands-On Learning

Students have the opportunity for hands-on learning experiences, allowing them to apply theoretical concepts in practical projects and assignments. Our experienced instructors provide guidance and support to help students develop essential skills and competencies in the field of computing.

Operating Hours

The computer lab is open during regular school hours and available for scheduled classes, and independent study sessions.
