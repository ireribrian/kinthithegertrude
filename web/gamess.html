Games at Our School

At our school, we believe that games and sports play a vital role in the overall development of students. They not only promote physical health but also teach teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. Participation in sports helps students to relieve stress and develop a sense of fair play and competitiveness.

Football

Football is one of the most popular sports at our school. It encourages teamwork and strategic thinking, while also providing a great cardiovascular workout. Our students regularly participate in inter-school tournaments and have a lot of enthusiasm for the game.

Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced sport that improves reflexes, hand-eye coordination, and stamina. It is enjoyed by many students, both recreationally and competitively. Our badminton courts are well-maintained to ensure a great playing experience.

Scrabble

Scrabble is not just a game; it is an educational tool that enhances vocabulary and cognitive skills. Students enjoy challenging each other in this classic word game, which also helps in developing strategic thinking and patience.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a dynamic sport that involves teamwork, coordination, and quick reflexes. Our school provides ample opportunities for students to engage in volleyball, helping them to stay active and develop their athletic abilities.

Table Tennis

Table Tennis is a popular indoor sport at our school. It enhances hand-eye coordination, concentration, and agility. Students love playing table tennis during their free time and often participate in friendly matches and school tournaments.
